Our Story

Founded in 2015, J-4 Pressure Wash has been serving the Winter Park community with pride and dedication. What started as a small family business has grown into the area's most trusted pressure washing service, built on a foundation of quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Our journey began when our founder, Mike Johnson, noticed a lack of reliable, professional pressure washing services in the Winter Park area. With a background in construction and a passion for helping homeowners maintain their properties, Mike set out to create a company that would set new standards for exterior cleaning services.

Today, we continue to uphold those same values: delivering outstanding results, using eco-friendly methods, and treating every customer like family. Our commitment to excellence has made us the go-to choice for homeowners and businesses throughout Winter Park and surrounding areas.
